Phrases such as “you”, “your” and other similar expressions refer to our customers, or the specific users of this Site. By visiting and using the Site or App, you accept the practices described in this Privacy Policy. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

Collected Information

When you place an order, submit your account information or participate in Kmart promotions or surveys, our App collects your personal information such as your name, e-mail address and mailing address and also device information such as device token, operating system version, location, manufacturer and model. We will not store your payment information related to your credit card numbers and banking accounts. Kmart will use your information collected online to process and fulfill your order. Additionally, your e-mail address and phone number may be collected at various points within the App so that we can send you any necessary e-mail messages and SMSs related to your order, such as order and shipping confirmations. We will also use your phone number to send One Time Password (OTP). In addition, we maintain a record of your purchase history, product interests, and whatever other details that might enable us to improve your shopping experience. Analytics such as App use and traffic patterns are also considered to help us improve the design of our website, the products we make and exclusive services we offer.

Cookies

Cookies are used to store information about user when visiting our website and this data is stored on your computer. We use Cookie information to identify and track visiting users. You can instruct your browser to disable cookies. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some of our services. As is common practice with almost all professional websites this site uses cookies, which are tiny files that are downloaded to your computer, to improve your experience. This page describes what information they gather, how we use it and why we sometimes need to store these cookies. We will also share how you can prevent these cookies from being stored however this may downgrade or 'break' certain elements of the sites functionality.

Site Statistics

We use Google analytics to collect and monitor user trends on our App and Website. Analytical data such as hits to our server, traffic patterns, actions and page views shows us where our audience is coming from and how they interact with us.
